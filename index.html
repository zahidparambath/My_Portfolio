BookSpot

A book lending management application, which connects users who want to lend books with those looking to borrow, promoting a sense of community among book enthusiasts. One user can upload the book if they want to lend it, and on the other side, the borrower can place a request to the lender for the book. This application is built using Flutter and firebase.
